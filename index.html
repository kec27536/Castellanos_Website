About Me:

Hello!

My name is Kevin Castellanos! I am currently a Senior Multimedia Production Student at California State University Northridge. During my time here at Northridge, I've discovered that I have an interest in creating media through different mediums such as video, audio, and graphic/web design!

I also love making Hip-Hop music. I have always loved music as a child... from my moms 80's music and cumbias, to my dad's disco. But it was my three older siblings, whom I hold dear to my heart, that showed me Hip-Hop music, and I've been hooked ever since. I also like to collect records, and hangout with my brothers from Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc!

I am coding this website 100% by myself by using programs and techniques that I've learned from my time at CSUN.

That means that you can find some of the videos, graphics, music, etc.. that I have created throughout the years!

Finally,

I've included a picture of my mother and I.

My mother is a superhero, and she is my main motivation! Without her, I wouldn't have had the opportunity to create things like a digital art portfolio. I love you mom!

Thank you for checking out my website!

-Kevin Castellanos
